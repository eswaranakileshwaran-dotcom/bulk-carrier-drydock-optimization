## 3. Revised project scope statement

> This case study models a Panamax bulk carrier's 20-year Class renewal
> (Special Survey) dry docking at a China repair yard, covering:
> steel renewal of 600–800 tons in way of tank top plating, bulkhead
> structure, and double bottom tanks; main engine and auxiliary engine
> overhaul; auxiliary equipment overhaul; 20-year statutory renewal of
> safety and fire-fighting equipment; and a 5-year-cycle hull coating
> (recoat, not full steel-life recoat). The yard's steel renewal
> production capacity (15–25 t/day) is treated as the binding constraint
> on dock duration, and is the key input carried into the process map,
> Gantt chart, and Arena discrete-event simulation.
